# Privacy Policy

**Offline Workout Tracker**
Last updated: June 9, 2026

## Overview

Offline Workout Tracker is designed to work entirely offline. Your workout data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

## Data Storage

All data (exercises, workouts, templates, and settings) is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. This data is never sent anywhere unless you explicitly choose to share or export it.

## Data Sharing

The app includes optional features to share workout templates with nearby devices via NFC or Wi-Fi Direct. These transfers happen directly between devices on a local connection. No data passes through any server.

## Data Export

You can export your data to a JSON file using the in-app export feature. This file is saved to a location you choose on your device. You control what happens with exported files.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, all used solely for the local device-to-device sharing feature:

- **NFC** — to share workout templates by tapping devices together
- **Wi-Fi / Nearby Devices / Location** — required by Android to discover and connect to nearby devices via Wi-Fi Direct. No location data is collected or stored.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses no third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
